About Me

BA Literature

With a B.A. in Literature and Science from the University of Hyderabad, I developed a strong foundation in critical thinking and interdisciplinary exploration—skills that deeply inform my approach to teaching Kuchipudi as both an art and a narrative science..

MA Integrated

Pursuing an M.A. in Literature and Science at the University of Hyderabad refined my ability to interpret complex narratives and cultural expressions—an insight that enriches my storytelling and emotive depth in Kuchipudi performance and pedagogy.

PhD

My Ph.D. in Dance deepened my scholarly and practical understanding of classical Indian performance, allowing me to approach Kuchipudi not just as an art form, but as a living tradition rooted in history, theory, and cultural dialogue.

Two decades of training. Taught 100+ students.

A vibrant space where the soul of Kuchipudi meets the spirit of innovation, creating dancers who embody both grace and flair.
